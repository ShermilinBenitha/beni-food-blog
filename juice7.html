CHAMPAGNE PUNCHR

INGREDIENTS

  • 1 ½ cups pineapple juice
  • 1 ½ cups orange juice
  • 1 750mL bottle champagne, chilled
  • 1 cup raspberries
  • 8 sprigs rosemary

INSTRUCTIONS

Using a pitcher, mix orange and pineapple juice. Pour orange-pineapple mixture into champagne flutes until half full. Fill glasses with champagne. Serve immediately, garnished with raspberries and rosemary, if desired.
