STRAWBERRY LEMONADE SMOOTHIE

INGREDIENTS

  • ½ cup sugar
  • ⅔ cup lemon juice
  • 3 cups frozen strawberries
  • ½ cup vanilla yogurt

INSTRUCTIONS

Combine sugar and 1/2 cup water in a medium saucepan over medium heat, stirring until the sugar has dissolved; let cool. In a large pitcher, whisk together sugar mixture, lemon juice and 1 1/2 cups water. Combine strawberries, yogurt, lemon mixture and 2 cups ice in blender until smooth. Serve immediately.
