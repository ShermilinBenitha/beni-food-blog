CRANBERRY MIMOSA

INGREDIENTS

  • 1 ¼ cups orange juice
  • 1 ¼ cups pineapple juice
  • ½ cup cranberry juice
  • 1 25.4-ounce bottle sparkling white wine, chilled
  • ½ cup cranberries
  • 8 sprigs fresh rosemary

INSTRUCTIONS

Using a pitcher, mix orange, pineapple and cranberry juice. Pour orange mixture into champagne flutes until half full. Fill glasses with white wine. Serve immediately, garnished with cranberries and rosemary, if desired.
