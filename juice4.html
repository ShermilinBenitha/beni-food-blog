STRAWBERRY LEMONADE BELLINI

INGREDIENTS

  • 3 cups frozen strawberries
  • 1 cup lemonade
  • 1 750-mL bottle Prosecco or other sparkling wine, chilled
  • 4 strawberries, halved

INSTRUCTIONS

Combine strawberries and lemonade in blender until smooth. Pour strawberry mixture into champagne flutes until half full. Fill glasses with Prosecco. Serve immediately, garnished with strawberry halves, if desired.
