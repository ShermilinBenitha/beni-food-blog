PINEAPPLE MINT SPRITZER

INGREDIENTS

  • 2 cups pineapple juice
  • 1 cup orange juice
  • 1 25.4-ounce bottle Moscato, chilled*
  • 8 sprigs fresh mint

INSTRUCTIONS

Using a pitcher, mix pineapple and orange juice. Pour pineapple-orange mixture into serving glasses until half full. Fill glasses with Moscato. Serve immediately, garnished with mint, if desired.
